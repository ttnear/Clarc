### New

- The memo and terminal panel can now be docked on the right (default) or at the bottom, configurable in Settings → General.
- A new "show both" option splits the panel so the memo and terminal are visible at once — stacked on the right, side by side at the bottom — each with a draggable resize handle.
- The terminal now uses a floating reset button and matches your active theme's colors.

### Fixed

- Resetting the terminal no longer changes the panel's split proportions.
- Cumulative response time is preserved when leaving a session and is no longer wiped by zero-duration saves.
